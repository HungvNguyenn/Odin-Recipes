Chocolate chip cookies are a simple and easy dessert to make and it only take a few ingredient and a bit of time to make

Ingedients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnut

Steps

  1. gather ingredient.
  2. preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Beat the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth
  3. Beat in egg, one at a time and then stir in vanilla
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water, Add to batter along with salt
  5. Stir in flour, and chocolate chip
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheet
  7. Bake in the oven until the edge are nicely brown, about 11-12 minutes
  8. Cool on the baking sheet before removing to a wire rack to cool completly
