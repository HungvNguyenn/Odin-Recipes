Lasagna recipe

Homemade lasagna can be pretty simple to make and is not imtimidating as long as you follow the recipe and take your time cooking them

The ingredient to making them is simple and it only take 3 hours to make, taking 1 hour 25 min to prep and 1 hour 15 min to cook

Ingedients

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 can crushed tomato
  • 2 tablespoons of chipped parsley
  • 1 clove crush garlic
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 3 large egg
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. gather ingredient.
  2. combine pork and ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  5. While the sauce simmer, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil, Cook the lasagna noodle until tender yet firm to bite, it should take around 8-10 minutes. Drain the water and set the noodle asside, then preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. Mix cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, egg, parsley, salt, and pepper ina large bowl until combine.
  7. Assemble lasagna: spread a spoon or two of sauce over the bottom of baking dish, then two layer of noodle over the sauce then cheese and then repeat until all the the ingredient are use.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminium foil. Baked in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, then remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown. It should take 5-10 more minutes
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes before cutting and serving
